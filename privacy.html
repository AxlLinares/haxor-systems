Privacy Policy

Data Protection & Security at HAXOR

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Data Controller

HAXOR, with headquarters in Puebla, Mexico, and web portal haxor.systems, is responsible for the use and protection of your personal data.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

The personal data we collect from you (Name, Company, Email, and Technical Challenges) will be used for the following purposes necessary for the requested service:

  • Perform preliminary technical diagnostics through Artificial Intelligence tools
  • Manage strategic consulting sessions and appointment scheduling
  • Prepare personalized commercial proposals and technical dossiers
  • Send relevant information about cybersecurity and IT infrastructure

3. Security & Discretion

At HAXOR, 'Quiet Luxury' extends to your privacy. We implement industrial-grade encryption protocols to ensure that your company's strategic information is not accessible to third parties.

We do not sell or share your database for external advertising purposes.

4. ARCO Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Know what personal data we have about you
  • Rectification: Understand what we use it for
  • Cancellation: Request its deletion
  • Opposition: Object to the use of your data for specific purposes

To exercise these rights, send a request to:

legal@haxor.systems

5. Consent

By using our diagnostic tools (Express Auditor, Cyber-Shield Scanner, ROI Calculator, and Use Cases Finder), you accept the processing of your data under the terms of this notice.

6. Data We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Name and company information
  • Email address
  • Company size and industry
  • Technical challenges and pain points
  • Current technology infrastructure details

Automatically Collected Data:

  • Browser type and language preferences
  • Time zone for regional detection
  • Usage patterns of diagnostic tools

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting legal@haxor.systems.

8. International Data Transfer

Your data may be processed on servers located outside of Mexico. We ensure that any international data transfer complies with applicable data protection regulations and maintains the same level of protection described in this policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us:
